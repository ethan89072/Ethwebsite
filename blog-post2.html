Top Cybersecurity Mistakes to Avoid

Published: August 2025

Many cyberattacks succeed not because hackers are too advanced but because of simple, avoidable mistakes...

Common Mistakes:

  • Using weak or reused passwords.
  • Ignoring software updates and patches.
  • Falling for phishing scams.
  • Lack of proper data backups.

By avoiding these errors, both individuals and businesses can greatly reduce their risk exposure...
